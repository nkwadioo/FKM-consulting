We are FMK

A company established with the sole purpose of providing people and business with accounting, taxation and auditing services to new/existing small and medium businesses, public entities and Municipalities in South Africa. Because we value professional relationships in any growing business, we offer professional services that empower you in achieving your business objectives, company strategy and ultimately drive growth effectively and efficiently.


The team comprise of qualified Chartered Accountants and junior accountants with experience in the areas of Accounting, Financial Management, Management Accounting, Auditing, Financial Governance, Taxation, Strategic and Business Management and coaching. The team shares a common vision and an interest in the development of people and the country. The team members have been exposed to both the private and public sector environments i.e. mining, public entities, SMMEs, manufacturing, and audit thereby being well rounded individuals with a holistic set of skills.
