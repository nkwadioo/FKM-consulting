Accounting & Tax Services

Accounting is a critical element to the effective governance of any organisation, be it in public, private or in the NGO sector. Reliable financial reports enable the board, council and management to make sound financial decisions that drive the organisation’s strategy.


Our accounting services include preparation of annual financial statements, preparation and review of monthly management accounts, bookkeeping, statutory registrations and statutory returns. We also provide onsite accountants and bookkeepers to assist clients in the preparation of their financial statements, in accordance with the client's needs.


Compliance with Tax legislation is critical in the financial management of entities through the avoidance of unnecessary penalties and interest. We provide clients with tailor-made services to ensure compliance with SARS obligations including VAT, PAYE, SDL, UIF and Income Tax.

Management Consulting Services

It is imperative for businesses to keep up-to-date on financial records to measure past performance and plan effectively for the future. We assist in developing and maintaining financial planning, analysing and reporting activities for the organization, as well as coordinating with relevant stakeholders in establishing budgets and their subsequent control and monitoring.


The ability to manage emerging risks effectively is one of the key characteristics differentiating the 'most effective' organisations from the rest. The challenges and experiences of the last global economic crisis taught us that managing risk for any business remains a vital requirement. Thus, the need to demonstrate probity and efficient management of resources becomes critical. There are four ways an organisation can deal with identified risks; accept, transfer, avoid or mitigate. The key to success lies in realising that risk is not something that should be avoided, more often than not, a risk also can represent an opportunity in disguise.
